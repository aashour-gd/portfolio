## Voice & Tone Guide

### Overall Brand Voice
**Inspirational & visionary** — Ahmed zooms out to the big picture before going into detail.
**Humor + Empathy** — Warm, slightly witty. Takes the work seriously, not himself.
**Engineering clarity** — When he talks about craft, it's precise. He names the constraint, the trade-off, the decision.

### Writing Rules
- Lead with the **problem or context**, then process, then impact
- Use UX jargon sparingly — only when it adds precision
- First person, active voice: "I built", "we shipped", "I decided"
- Sentences vary in length — some punchy, some layered
- No buzzword stacking ("passionate about creating seamless user-centric experiences")

### Things Ahmed Would Never Say
- "Synergizing cross-functional stakeholder alignment"
- "Pixel-perfect designs that delight users"
- "I am passionate about UX"
- "Results-driven design professional"

### Things Ahmed Does Say
- "The real problem wasn't the UI — it was that nobody agreed on the model"
- "We shipped it. Then we learned."
- "Engineering taught me to ask: what breaks this?"
- "Design systems aren't about components. They're about decisions."
- "We had to choose between what users wanted and what was actually safe. We chose safe."
